Explored practical usage of ML method via [Pix2Pix](https://www.tensorflow.org/tutorials/generative/pix2pix) , where we trained data on dancers and their corresponding movements using Posenet through Processing. The trained data allow us to create images and video rendering providing Posenet positions. This allows for automated creation to live-demo of dances with different avatar on the trained data.
